Welcome to Science Kids Center!

From a grain of sand to the vast galaxy, science uncovers secrets around you and me. Science has been a basis of many discoveries in society. Whether it be the history or looking ahead in technological advance, the love of science is something that should be embraced by all.

At Science Kids center, we accompany our children to discover the wonders of science and inspire them to imagine and invent from a young age. Kids love to ask questions and wonder about the answers! Our mission is to walk our little Einsteins through this process of discovery by doing fun experiments together. The science of today is the technology of tomorrow and the best way to learn is by doing!

Why be a Science Kid?

Children don't always know why they do science. In fact, sometimes they don't even know what they are doing is actually science. Mixing dirt and water to make mud; taking things apart and putting them back together; mixing flour and water to make bread; even just questioning about the rainbow in the sky all relate back to science.

Sometimes, even adults don't understand how common science is in our daily lives. We use chemistry and its concepts all the time without realizing them. For example, if you are trying to remove stains from your shirt, you are actually using chemical reactions to help you solve that problem.

Experiments and demonstrations help children to understand why something happens. At Science Kids center, we have gathered a series of fun and interesting experiments to ignite your little one's interest in science. We believe that children learn much more when they do experiments hands-on by themselves and have fun while doing so.
